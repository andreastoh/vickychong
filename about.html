VICKY CHONG graduated with Master of Arts - Creative Writing from Goldsmiths' College, University of London. Her creative non-fiction stories were published in two anthologies by Singapore's National Library Board.

Short story The Uber Driver won a prize in the 2018 Nick Joaquin Literary Awards Asia-Pacific. She had three short stories published in Business Mirror, Philippines.

Her short stories are found in the anthologies: The Best Asian Short Stories 2021, Letter to my Son and A View of Stars. She is the author of Racket and Other Stories, by Penguin Random House in 2021.
